Our Featured Brands

Discover skincare solutions from dermatologist-recommended brands

The Ordinary

The Ordinary revolutionizes skincare through its "clinical formulations with integrity" philosophy. This Deciem brand cuts through industry hype to deliver single-ingredient, evidence-based solutions at remarkably affordable prices. Each product clearly lists its active ingredients and concentration percentages, empowering consumers with transparency rarely seen in skincare. The Ordinary's no-nonsense approach focuses on what actually works - whether it's their bestselling Niacinamide 10% + Zinc 1% for blemish-prone skin or Hyaluronic Acid 2% + B5 for intense hydration. By eliminating unnecessary fragrances, luxury packaging, and inflated marketing costs, they make advanced skincare actives accessible to everyone. Their products are designed to be mixed and matched, allowing users to create personalized regimens tailored to their specific skin concerns.

CeraVe

CeraVe is a dermatologist-developed skincare brand that revolutionized moisturization with its patented MVE Delivery Technology. Their formulas are enriched with three essential ceramides (1, 3, 6-II) that help restore and maintain the skin's natural protective barrier. Developed with dermatologists, CeraVe products combine these vital ceramides with cholesterol and fatty acids in the optimal ratio found naturally in healthy skin. The brand's unique MultiVesicular Emulsion (MVE) technology ensures continuous 24-hour release of these key ingredients. CeraVe's fragrance-free, non-comedogenic formulas are clinically proven to help retain moisture, strengthen the skin barrier, and are recommended for all skin types including sensitive skin. From their iconic Moisturizing Cream to the Foaming Facial Cleanser, each product is designed to deliver therapeutic results without unnecessary additives.

La Roche-Posay

Trusted by over 90,000 dermatologists worldwide, La Roche-Posay is a clinically-proven skincare brand rooted in pharmaceutical expertise. Developed with dermatologists, their formulas feature carefully selected active ingredients like thermal spring water rich in selenium and their signature antioxidant Neurosensine. Each product undergoes rigorous clinical testing on sensitive skin, with many formulations containing minimal ingredients to reduce irritation risk. The brand's commitment to dermatological excellence is showcased in their Effaclar line for acne-prone skin and Toleriane range for sensitive complexions. La Roche-Posay partners directly with dermatologists to develop products that address specific skin concerns while maintaining exceptional tolerance, making it a top recommended brand in clinical practices globally.
